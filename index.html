What We Do

Delta Force is the official programming club of NIT Trichy. We are responsible for the development and maintenance of our institute's official website as well as handling all web-related activities for the institute's cultural festival - Festember and technical festival - Pragyan. As a club, we work together and learn from each other, all the time exploring new projects and ideas.
